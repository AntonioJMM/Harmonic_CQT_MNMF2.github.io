Multichannel Blind Music Source Separation using Directivity-aware MNMF with Harmonicity Constraints


This page contains separation results for article "Multichannel Blind Music Source Separation using Directivity-aware MNMF with Harmonicity Constraints" submitted to IEEE Access.

Authors: Antonio J. Munoz-Montoro, Julio J. Carabias-Orti, Pablo Cabanas-Molero, Francisco J. Canadas-Quesada and Nicolas Ruiz-Reyes

Datasets

The experimental evaluation was carried out on the University of Rochester Multimodal Music Performance (URMP) dataset.
